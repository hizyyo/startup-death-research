## Batch Design

Batch 006 was intentionally small and gap-driven.

Primary goals:

- finish the 100-case MVP dataset
- avoid adding generic PMF/no-urgent-pain cases
- improve marketplace liquidity coverage
- improve regulatory/legal and trust/safety coverage
- add more non-US cases
- include a few non-software or hardware-adjacent failures to stress-test the taxonomy

## Strongest Cases In This Batch

- Cuddli: strong example of usage without sustainable business model.
- Community Coders: clear education marketplace supply-demand imbalance.
- Freshconnect: strong Indian agritech operations and marketplace case.
- Benja: rare founder-first account of legal/ethical failure.
- YOLO: useful trust/safety plus platform-dependency case.
- Bluesmart: clear hardware/regulatory failure caused by airline battery rules.

## Medium-Confidence Cases

- Bluesmart: strong failure mechanism, but main dataset source is secondary.
- YOLO: strong trust/safety and platform cutoff pattern, but not a traditional founder postmortem.

## Quality Notes

- Batch 006 deliberately includes a few borderline cases to stress-test the taxonomy across offline, hardware, legal, and trust/safety failures.
- The final dataset is not perfectly balanced, but it is broad enough to support meaningful analysis across categories.
- The next step should not be blindly adding more cases. The next step should be audit, cleanup, analysis, and public presentation.
